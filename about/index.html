About Us

The Arabic word “Iwan” means Hall.

Iwan Is found in many of the marvelous mosques around the world.

it is serving a number of functions including, prayer, teaching, lodging of teachers and students, keeping books and as a reading library as well as a number of charitable activities in stopping stations on the road of pilgrims intended to serve the ill and the needy.

More importantly it became the area in which traditional circles of knowledge formed and classes would gather to pass down the sacred sciences, generation to generation.

Even today many great mosques around the world hold classes under their “Iwans.”

Iwan Academy is an educational online academy in order to serve online Qur’an, Tajweed, Arabic Language, and Islamic Studies to Arabs & non-Arabic speakers; with an interesting technique in teaching through online one-to-one sessions, we can work with your needs to ensure the best learning environment for you or your kids.

Our Tutors

we know that learning is easier when you have an excellent teacher. Our instructors are native Egyptian Tutors qualified and graduated from Al-Azhar Al-Sharif University and they are passionate about the courses they teach and bring this enthusiasm into their classes and students.
